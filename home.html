Made with a VGG architecture

VGG is a standard deep convolutional neural network (CNN) wich consist of very small convolutional filters (3x3). For this particular project VGG19 was used.

TransferLearning and FineTuning

The base model is VGG19 trained on Imagenet, to wich a new head consisting of an additional convolutional layer, maxpooling, batchnormalization and two Fully conected layers were added.
